Chickpea Salad

Description

A vegetarian version of the classic chicken salad. Chickpeas are mashed and then combined with chopped vegetables and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 2 (15 oz) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced dill pickles
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt or mayonnaise
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons freshly chopped dill
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Kosher salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Place the chickpeas in a large bowl and mash with a fork or potato masher until most of the chickpeas are smashed. Stir in the celery, onion, and pickles.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt, mustard, dill, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the chickpea mixture and stir until well combined. Taste and adjust ingredients, if necessary.
