Vegetarian Turkish Pasta

Description

A pasta dish made with noodles, spiced lentils, garlicy greek yogurt sauce, paprika butter, tomatoes and parsley. Once the pasta, paprika butter, and lentils are cooked stir the garlic, salt, and lemon juice into the greek yogurt. Then all that's left to do is put some of each ingredient into your bowl and stir together.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Uncooked Green or Black Lentils rinsed
  • 1/2 White Onion chopped
  • 3 Tbsp ulive Oil
  • 1/2 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt or more, to taste
  • 2 Cups Plain, Unsweetened Yogurt
  • 2 Cloves Garlic grated
  • 2 Tbsp Freshly-Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt or more, to taste
  • 8 Tbsp Salted Butter
  • 1 Tsp Hot Paprika
  • 1 Lb Pasta
  • 2 Cups Cherry Tomatoes chopped
  • 1 Bunch Parsley chopped

Steps

  1. To begin, rinse your lentils thoroughly. Cook them in salted water according to your package's instructions. You should be left with about 2 1/2 – 3 cups cooked lentils.
  2. Bring the olive oil to medium heat in a large sauté pan. Add in the chopped white onion, and sauté for about 2-3 minutes. Then, add in the lentils and spices, and sauté for about 5 minutes, or until lentils have crisped a little in the oil.
  3. Next, cook the pasta of your choosing in salted water until al dente. If not serving immediately, rinse the pasta in cold water after draining to stop the cooking process.
  4. While the pasta is cooking, make the garlic yogurt. In a bowl, add in the yogurt, and grate 2 cloves of garlic directly into the yogurt. Add in the salt, lemon juice, and herbs, and mix. Taste, and adjust seasonings to your preference.
  5. To make the paprika butter, melt the salted butter in a small saucepan and add in the paprika. Once the butter is completely melted, turn off the heat and mix the paprika into the butter for about 1-2 minutes, to bloom the spice. Be careful not to brown your butter!
  6. Lastly, chop up your tomatoes and parsley.
  7. To assemble, have each person put a portion of pasta in their bowl. Top the pasta with a dollop of the yogurt sauce, then layer on the sautéed lentils, tomatoes, and parsley. Drizzle everything with the paprika butter. There are no set ratios – just add everything to your own preference!
